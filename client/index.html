About RuralCare360

RuralCare360 is a comprehensive telemedicine platform bridging the healthcare gap in rural Nigeria. We provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services to communities with limited access to medical facilities. Our platform connects patients with certified healthcare professionals, enabling remote consultations, digital health records, and continuous care management.
